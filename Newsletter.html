PUP Graduates: Top Choice for Employers

With a staggering 20% preference rate from employers, PUP graduates emerge as the top choice in the eyes of employers, as unveiled by JobStreet's recent report.

On June 29, 2023, Pia Regalado from SPOT.ph reported that according to JobStreet by SEEK, around 20% of employers in the Philippines prefer to hire graduates from the Polytechnic University of the Philippines (PUP), making it one of the top 10 preferred schools among hirers. The country manager of JobStreet by SEEK, Philip Gioca, emphasized that while individual qualities remain crucial in the hiring process, understanding hirer preferences can foster better connections between jobseekers and employers while also highlighting the strengths of different academic institutions across industries. PUP, with 23.39% of the vote, excels in being seen as effective team players in the workplace.

PUP Graduate Tops October 2019 Psychometrician Board Exams

Bernadette Alice Cruz Rigor, an alumna of PUP, has secured the top spot in the October 2019 psychometrician licensure examination.

In a remarkable display of academic excellence, Bernadette Alice Cruz Rigor, an alumna of the Polytechnic University of the Philippines (PUP), emerged as the topnotcher in the October 2019 psychometrician licensure examination, as reported by ABS-CBN News. The Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) revealed that out of 10,670 examinees, 6,800 successfully passed the rigorous examination. Bernadette's outstanding achievement was marked by an impressive rating of 86.80 percent.

PUP Psychology Student Awarded
Fulbright Scholarship

Alongside Jam Lopez as the first representative from PUP in the Fulbright Global Ugrad Program is Rachel Mecaela Baxal from PUP - Maragondon Branch, taking BS Accountancy. Together with the undergraduates is another representative from PUP, Dr. Lourdes V. Alvarez who is awarded the Fulbright Visiting Senior Scholarship Program.

On June 22, 2023, Fulbright Philippines held the reception ceremony in honor of the 2023-2024 Filipino grantees of Fulbright Philippines. Among the awardees is Jessica Jamimah ‘Jam’ Lopez, a third-year BS Psychology student from the College of Social Sciences and Development. She is awarded the prestigious Fulbright Philippines Global Undergraduate Exchange Program which will enable her to study for one semester at a U.S. University. The Global Undergraduate program chooses outstanding students from around the world to gain personal and professional development and cultural enrichment. Jam is chosen as 1 of the 9 successful candidates from the original pool of 519 applicants in the Philippines. A 1.7% chance of getting in.
