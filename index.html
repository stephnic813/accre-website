On June 29, 2023, Pia Regalado from SPOT.ph reported that according to JobStreet by SEEK, around 20% of employers in the Philippines prefer to hire graduates from the Polytechnic University of the Philippines (PUP), making it one of the top 10 preferred schools among hirers. The country manager of JobStreet by SEEK, Philip Gioca, emphasized that while individual qualities remain crucial in the hiring process, understanding hirer preferences can foster better connections between jobseekers and employers while also highlighting the strengths of different academic institutions across industries. PUP, with 23.39% of the vote, excels in being seen as effective team players in the workplace.
