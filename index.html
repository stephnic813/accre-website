PUP Psychology Student Awarded
Fullbright Scholarship

Alongside Jam Lopez as the first representative from PUP in the Fulbright Global Ugrad Program is Rachel Mecaela Baxal from PUP - Maragondon Branch, taking BS Accountancy. Together with the undergraduates is another representative from PUP, Dr. Lourdes V. Alvarez who is awarded the Fulbright Visiting Senior Scholarship Program.

On June 22, 2023, Fulbright Philippines held the reception ceremony in honor of the 2023-2024 Filipino grantees of Fulbright Philippines. Among the awardees is Jessica Jamimah ‘Jam’ Lopez, a third-year BS Psychology student from the College of Social Sciences and Development. She is awarded the prestigious Fulbright Philippines Global Undergraduate Exchange Program which will enable her to study for one semester at a U.S. University. The Global Undergraduate program chooses outstanding students from around the world to gain personal and professional development and cultural enrichment. Jam is chosen as 1 of the 9 successful candidates from the original pool of 519 applicants in the Philippines. A 1.7% chance of getting in.

Getting this feat was not an easy task. As a second-time applicant, she has experienced the disappointment of not getting in and the fulfillment of getting accepted. The rigorous process that she successfully accomplished is an example of how we should continue striving for our goals despite some defeats along the way. She is set to fly to the United States on August 24, 2023, to take her Fall semester at the University of Wyoming, Laramie, United States. Jam Lopez is an advocate for science and technology that genuinely serves the people. She also believes that appropriate education in our education system and social awareness is vital in achieving a better nation.
