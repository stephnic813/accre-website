Research

The Bachelor of Science in Psychology Program (BSPP) is a vital unit of the University adhering rigorously to the University Research Agenda. Research in the Department has become an integral part of every faculty member's discipline and, ultimately, their respective personal and professional cultures.

The Program is committed to the University’s mission of embedding a culture of research and innovation to achieve the University goal of ensuring inclusiveness and being a catalyst of change in society. It likewise adheres to the Ten Pillars of the University, more particularly, Pillar 6: Vigorous Research Production and Utilization. It adopts the approved University Research Manual as its core manual to ensure its research outputs are guided by the University's Vision, Mission, Goals, and Objectives. The strict adherence to the Research Manual in turn, guides the faculty members in producing a significant number of relevant and timely studies.
